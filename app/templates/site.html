FAQ

Got questions about Buykori? We've got answers.

Simple answers for server-side tracking, COD order management and courier fulfillment.

What is Buykori AdSync?

A connected WooCommerce platform for server-side ad tracking, COD purchase control and courier order management.

Can I manage orders from the dashboard?

Yes. You can review COD orders, submit consignments to supported couriers and follow delivery statuses from the portal.

Which couriers are supported?

The current order workflow supports Steadfast and Pathao booking with delivery status updates.

Does courier status affect purchase tracking?

Yes. Deferred purchase control helps align final purchase signals with the order status used by your COD workflow.

Does it support one-page landing pages?

Yes. One-page mode can wait for real checkout intent before sending InitiateCheckout.

Can I use only TikTok or only Meta?

Yes. Platform toggles can control which destination receives events for each store.

What data improves event match quality?

Content IDs, value, currency, click IDs, user agent, IP and hashed customer fields help platforms match events better.
